## 💡 The Concept: Data Analytics Beyond Business
Data analysis isn't just for e-commerce conversion rates or sports metrics; it can optimize our daily routines. This project is an interactive **Nutritional Prototype** built to simulate the physiological impact of natural extracts compared to commercial energy drinks.

Instead of traditional KPIs, this interactive dashboard acts as a "Custom Blend Simulator." It uses a JavaScript-based engine to analyze the real-time impact of adjusting active ingredients on Focus, Immunity, and Metabolism.

## 🧪 The Formulation Engine
The simulation is built around four core natural extracts:
* **Cinnamon:** Evaluated for its impact on metabolic rates.
* **Ginger:** Modeled for its immunity-boosting properties.
* **Fenugreek:** Analyzed for blood sugar regulation and sustained energy without the crash.
* **Turmeric:** Scored for its antioxidant payload and cognitive focus extension.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Interactive Sliders:** Users can act as "formulators," adjusting milligram (mg) dosages of each extract.
* **Synthetic Simulation Engine:** A custom Vanilla JS engine instantly recalculates efficacy scores based on the input ratios.
* **Dynamic Radar Chart:** Visually compares the custom natural blend against a standard benchmark (a high-sugar, synthetic caffeine energy drink) across 5 performance vectors.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **HTML5 & Vanilla JS:** For structure and the simulation logic engine.
* **Tailwind CSS:** For a clean, modern, and responsive UI (Lab Mode).
* **Chart.js:** For the dynamic Radar and Bar visualizations.
